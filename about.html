Who We Are

We are a group of individuals who all lead very different but very active lifestyles. We prioritize healthy eating, but are often frustrated by our lack of options. Salads are great, but we find ourselves feeling hungry again less than an hour later. We made HYP for the people like us.

Our Mission

As a team, we want to help people overcome the issues that we face everyday by making healthy, and fulfilling eating easier and more accessible to everyone.

What We Made

The solution that we divised is a salad topping or dip that is jampacked with 10g of protein per serving plus all the other good stuff like gluten free, non-gmo, organic and vegan.
