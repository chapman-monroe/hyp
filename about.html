The Problem

Salads are great, and we should all eat more veggies, but not when they have you reaching for extra snacks because they never filled you up in the first place. We were frustrated by the lack of options on the market to make our salads both delicious and filling without unnecessary fats and sugars. Thatâ€™s why we made HYP, for people like us and people like you.

The Solution

HYP was made from conversation. We talked to nutritionists, fitness experts, and most importantly our consumers to understand what a perfect dressing would look like. What we came up with was an organic, non-GMO, gluten free, and vegetarian salad dressing that packs 10g of protein per serving.
